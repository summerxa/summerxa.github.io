Alphabet Interpreter

Jun-Aug 2023

Created by Anne Xia as an independent project.

Android app written in Java, utilizes AI to identify ASL alphabet signs from live camera feed.

Training data was various photos of hands taken from online databases. Model connected OpenCV, MediaPipe, and 4-layer fully-connected neural network and was trained to classify hand gesture images as letters. Used Python to train and test model, integrated final model into mobile app.
